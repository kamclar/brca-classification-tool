## VUS problematika

**Culver et al. 2013** — Variants of Uncertain Significance in BRCA Testing: Evaluation of Surgical Decisions, Risk Perception, and Cancer Distress
- [PMC3751990](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC3751990/)

**Eccles et al. 2015** — Understanding of BRCA VUS genetic results by breast cancer specialists
- [PMC4660681](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC4660681/)

- [Nature](https://www.nature.com/articles/s41586-024-08388-8)

**Shimelis et al. 2022** — Classification of BRCA2 VUS Using an ACMG/AMP Model Incorporating HDR Functional Assay
